# Карта источников и тем

## Рамки и словари

- WEF: human-centric skills, core skills, AI-era skill shifts and Future of Jobs.
- OECD: social and emotional skills, distinction between skills and personality traits, outcomes and measurement.
- ESCO: transversal knowledge, skills and competences as labor-market classifier.
- LifeComp: personal, social and learning-to-learn competences.
- Skills Development Scotland / SQA: meta-skills as higher-order transferable capabilities.
- NACE, SHRM, Burning Glass Institute, LinkedIn, PwC: skills-first hiring, AI-exposed jobs, changing skill profiles.

## Механизмы

- Executive functions: inhibition, working memory, cognitive flexibility as basis for self-regulation.
- Metacognition: planning, monitoring, evaluation, calibration of confidence and learning loops.
- Self-regulated learning: goal setting, strategy use, feedback, reflection and transfer.
- Social cognition: intention reading, trust, coordination, norm-following and status/prestige learning.
- Emotion regulation and resilience: maintaining useful behavior under stress, uncertainty and conflict.
- Sense-making: turning ambiguous contexts into shared models and actionable next steps.

## Эволюционные линии

- Social brain hypothesis.
- Cooperative breeding.
- Interdependence hypothesis and human cooperation.
- Natural pedagogy and cultural transmission.
- Prestige bias, norm psychology and cultural-gene coevolution.
- Self-domestication and reduced reactive aggression.

## Оценка и развитие

- Self-report: cheap and scalable, but vulnerable to desirability and weak behavioral validity.
- Structured interview: strong method when standardized and behaviorally anchored.
- Work samples and simulations: closer to real behavior, but costly and context-dependent.
- SJT: useful for judgment in social/work scenarios, but requires careful design.
- 360/peer feedback and artifacts: useful for triangulation.
- Deliberate practice, coaching, apprenticeship, simulation-based learning and reflective practice: practical pathway for development.

# Карта источников и тем

## Тематические блоки

- GTD: capture, clarify, next action, contexts, weekly review.
- Personal Kanban: visualize work, limit WIP, manage flow.
- Kanban method: explicit policies, classes of service, flow, WIP limits.
- Goal-setting theory: goals, feedback, commitment and specificity.
- Self-determination theory: autonomy, competence, relatedness as motivational substrate.
- Interruption and context switching: attention residue, task switching, cost of interruptions in knowledge work.
- Psychological detachment: boundaries, recovery and work-home transition.
- Burnout: occupational phenomenon, overload and failed recovery.
- Team productivity: psychological safety, collective intelligence, great teamwork conditions.
- DORA/DevOps: flow, deployment frequency, lead time, change failure and recovery metrics.
- Meetings and coordination: productive meetings, decision logs, async practices and meeting hygiene.

## Практические модели

- Личный контур: inbox -> clarify -> next action -> calendar/deep work -> waiting/backlog -> weekly review.
- Портфель сфер: work, health, family, learning, finances, recovery; для каждой сферы нужен minimum viable maintenance.
- Командный контур: visible board -> WIP limits -> blockers -> explicit policies -> metrics -> retrospective.
- AI-assisted planning: extraction of next actions, overload detection, weekly review prompts, dependency mapping, meeting summaries.
